Research on how consumers perceive, evaluate, and adopt battery electric vehicles (BEVs) and alternative fuel vehicles (AFVs). Current work at GWU and NIST applies stated preference methods and conjoint analysis to quantify willingness-to-pay for used vehicle battery health information and other EV-related attributes.
